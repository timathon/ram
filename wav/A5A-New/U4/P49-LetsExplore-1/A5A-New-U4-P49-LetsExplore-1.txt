总结全文
这三句名言共同传达了一个主题：成功源于坚持不懈的行动，从小处着手，不断尝试，即使失败也要继续前进并从中进步。
These three quotes collectively convey a theme: success comes from persistent action, starting small, continuously trying, and even when failing, keep moving forward and improving from it.
